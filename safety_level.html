The view is broken down by Table Name vs. Airline. The view is filtered on Table Name, which keeps 6 of 6 members. Airlines are categorized based on safety level. The level are created as below.

  • A-safety - includes airlines which have no incident, no fatal accidents and no fatalities.
  • B-safety - includes airlines which have incidents, but no fatal accidents and no fatalities.
  • C-safety - includes airlines which have fatalities less than 10.
  • D-safety - includes airlines which have fatalities less than 50.
  • E-safety - includes airlines which have fatalities less than 100.
  • F-safety - includes airlines which have fatalities more than 100.
