Passenger by Geo Region

The monthly passenger traffic statistics by airline was retrieved from Data SF. Out of all the geographical regions compared, US airlines had the highest number of passengers. It was obvious that more than half (56%) of the total passengers travelled in US airlines to and from San Francisco International Airport for the years compared, 2005 to 2018.
