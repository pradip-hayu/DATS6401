Individual Revenue (2003-2016)

  • American: American airlines had the highest revenue of 41.08 billion dollars in 2015. In 2009, there was a sudden decrease in its revenue, but it picked up progressively. In 2015, there was a massive increase in its revenue from 27.14 to 41.08 billion dollars.
  • Continental: Continental airlines had the highest revenue of 16.17 billion dollars in 2011. Interesting fact: it merged with the parent company of United Airlines in 2010.
  • Delta: The revenue of delta airlines was increasing up until 2008. Its revenue decreased in 2009 from 20.97 to 18.05 billion dollars. In 2010, it picked up its revenue again with an amount of 31.89 billion. The revenue increased gradually afterwards with the highest revenue of 40.82 billion in 2015.
